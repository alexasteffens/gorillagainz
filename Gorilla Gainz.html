Meet the Coaches!



Coach Powell

Coach Powell officially launched Gorilla Gainz in 2020 in his hometown, Morristown, New Jersey. He is a Former NFL defensive back who played on the Chicago bears and the Washington Commanders, along with mutiple other professional football leagues including the XFL and the CFL. He attended Widener University where he was a captain and was named Conference Defensive Player of the year in 2017, and back to back 1st Team All American for divison 3 football. Coach Powell spent the majority of his NFL preparation at the olympic training facility in Denver colordo where he obtained his knowldge and expertise from some of the best coaches and trainers in the profession. Functional Strength and condition, injury prevention, and sports specfic training are some of the main concepts that coach powell focuses on with his clients. Coach Powell has trained a variety of different clients from professional athelets, adults who are trying to start their fitness journey, and small children who are just trying to get active with some quailty excerise in their daily life. Coach Powell prides himself on getting the best out of his clients and making sure that their fitess journey is fun and enjoyable while reaching their goals!

Coach Jordan

Coach Jordan is a former dual-sport collegiate athlete. He was an All-Conference Defensive Back and Team Captain on the Widener University Football Team and helped the Track & Field team win multiple conference championships during his time in college. In college, he earned his business degree while also aquiring his Personal Training Certification, and he is currently specilizing in Corrective Exercise & Performance Enhancement. His approach to fitness is to correct any muscle imbalances by first fixing static and dynamic posture, then isolating underactive muscles, and finally developing efficent, integrated, movement patterns. Once all muscle imbalances are eliminated, Coach Jordan will then work to develop a strong foundation of joint stability and strength within each client to prepare them for impact from sports, heavy explosive movements, or regular everday life. Coach Jordan truly enjoys helping his clients reach their fitness goals, eliminate chronic pain, and gain confidence and a postivie outlook on life.
